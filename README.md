# MyFate

**MyFate** is a student-first academic decision clarity platform designed to help learners understand the real consequences of academic choices *before* those consequences happen. Instead of guessing how a missed deadline, late penalty, or high-weight assignment might affect their future, **MyFate** provides a clear, transparent, and fully manual way to model outcomes and plan ahead.

## ğŸ§® Assignment Impact Analysis

At its core, **MyFate** allows students to analyze how individual assignments affect their overall course performance.

Students manually input:
- Course and subject (searchable dropdowns)
- Assignment type (exam, project, homework, midterm, final, etc.)

- The impact of late penalties

Results are presented in a clear, readable format designed to support real decision-making.
